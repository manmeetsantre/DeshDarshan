2 Days in Delhi: Red Fort & Mughal Heritage

This 2-day itinerary explores the majestic Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the political hub of Mughal India. Discover the grandeur of Mughal architecture and the vibrant culture of Old Delhi.

Trip Highlights

  • Red Fort complex exploration
  • Chandni Chowk market visit
  • Old Delhi street food tour
  • Jama Masjid visit
  • Museums within Red Fort

Day 1: Red Fort & Old Delhi

9:00 AM

Red Fort Exploration

Begin at the Lahore Gate, walk through Chatta Chowk bazaar, and explore the Diwan-i-Aam, Diwan-i-Khas, Rang Mahal, and other structures within this massive fort complex.

12:00 PM

Lunch at Karim's

Enjoy legendary Mughlai cuisine at this century-old restaurant near Jama Masjid, famous for its kebabs and biryanis.

2:00 PM

Jama Masjid

Visit India's largest mosque, built by Shah Jahan. Climb the minaret for panoramic views of Old Delhi.

4:00 PM

Chandni Chowk Walk

Explore the bustling lanes of Old Delhi's main market, visiting spice markets, cloth shops, and historic havelis.

Day 2: Museums & Light Show

10:00 AM

Red Fort Museums

Visit the archaeological museum and war memorial museum within the fort complex to learn about Delhi's history.

1:00 PM

Lunch at Haldiram's

Experience authentic North Indian vegetarian cuisine at this iconic restaurant.

3:00 PM

National Museum

Explore artifacts from India's 5000-year history at this premier museum near India Gate.

7:00 PM

Red Fort Light Show

End your trip with the spectacular sound and light show that narrates Delhi's history (check timings as per season).

Photo Gallery

Travel Tips for Red Fort

Best Time to Visit: October to March. The light show timings vary by season.

Tickets: Separate tickets for fort entry and museums. Friday closed.

Dress Comfortably: Wear comfortable shoes as there's lots of walking.

Photography: The fort looks spectacular in morning light. Tripods may require permission.
